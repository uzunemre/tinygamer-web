3 Credits & Purchases

3.1 What are Credits?

Credits are the in-app currency used to generate games. They are a consumption unit, not a game count. The number of credits deducted per game generation depends on the length and complexity of your prompt:

  • 1–100 characters: 1 Credit
  • 101–300 characters: 3 Credits
  • 301+ characters: 7 Credits

3.2 Purchasing Credits

Credits are purchased through the Apple App Store using in-app purchase packages. All transactions are processed by Apple and are subject to Apple's payment terms. We do not collect or store your payment information directly.

3.3 Non-Refundable Policy

Please Note Credits are non-refundable and have no monetary value outside of the App. Credits consumed during a failed generation attempt will be refunded to your account automatically. Credits do not expire.

3.4 Restore Purchases

If you reinstall the App or switch devices, you can restore previously purchased credits using the "Restore Purchases" feature in the App, provided you are signed in with the same Apple ID.
